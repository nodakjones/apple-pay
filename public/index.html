Apple Pay Demo with CardPointe

Test Controls

Amount: USD

Testing Instructions

  1. This demo must be accessed via Safari on macOS or iOS
  2. The page must be served over HTTPS (using ngrok or similar)
  3. Click "Check Apple Pay Availability" to verify your browser supports Apple Pay
  4. If available, an Apple Pay button will appear
  5. Click the Apple Pay button to initiate a payment
  6. Complete the payment in the Apple Pay sheet
  7. Check the status area for the result
